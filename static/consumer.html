LPG SAFETY PROTOCOL

If you have a damaged cylinder, suspect a gas leak, or smell gas:

  1. Extinguish all open flames/fires. Do NOT light matches or lighters.
  2. Do NOT operate electrical switches, appliances, or phone inside the area.
  3. Turn off the cylinder regulator switch immediately.
  4. Open all doors and windows to ventilate the property.
  5. Evacuate all occupants and call the 24x7 Emergency Line 1906 immediately!
Frequently Asked Questions
What should I do in case of a gas leak?
Safety first! Turn off the regulator immediately, open all doors/windows, do NOT touch electrical switches, and vacate the area. Call the national emergency helpline 1906 immediately. Do not submit a support ticket for active gas leak emergency services.
What is the standard delivery timeline (SLA)?
Refill cylinders are usually delivered within 24 to 48 hours of booking confirmation. You can check the live status of your booking under the 'My Bookings' tab.
Why was my booking cancelled?
Bookings are automatically cancelled if they violate quota regulations, if the minimum gap between refills has not been reached, or due to payment verification failures. You can link your cancelled booking to a support ticket to inquire about refunds.
How does the delivery verification work?
Upon delivery, show the QR code from the 'My QR Codes' tab to the delivery agent. Once the agent scans the QR code, the status updates to 'Delivered' automatically.
